What is data analysis?
Data analysis is the process of processing information to identify patterns, trends, and make decisions. It involves collecting, cleaning, visualizing, and interpreting data.

Why is it important?
Today, data is everywhere — from business to science. Its analysis helps companies understand customers, reduce costs, predict events, and quickly respond to changes.

Where is it used?
1. Business — companies analyze customer behavior and optimize advertising (e.g., Amazon and Netflix).
2. Medicine — disease prognosis, treatment selection, epidemic analysis.
3. Finance — risk assessment, fraud prevention, investment decisions.
